DevDesk Queue

Welcome to DevDesk Queue

At this site you can request help as well as help others through our ticketing system. Requestors can submit tickets as well as check on the status of their ticket. Helpers can pull tickets from the queue, add helpful notes and close tickets, as well as return tickets to the queue if they are unable to solve the issue.

About

This site was designed for Web Part-time 3 through 5's first build week. It was designed to solve a real need to track and route support tickets for students needing assistance from PMs. It will hopefully be able to help future students in the future.
